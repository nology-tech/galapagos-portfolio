We are Two Headed Duck

We are a 'translation collective' specialising in novellas, short-story collections, and books of essays from throughout the Chinese-speaking world.

Our network consists of highly talented translators, editors and researchers. Our specialist collaborative workflow enables us to produce high-quality translations of challenging and engaging texts.

Jack Hargreaves

Founder and lead translator

Jack Hargreaves is a writer and Chinese-English translator based in the UK. Specialising in literary and academic writing, Jack has translated more than one million Chinese characters, including Buddhist, historical and philosophical works, creative non-fiction by Ye Duoduo and Yuan Ling and a variety of fiction. Forthcoming translations include Li Juan’s Winter Pasture, Yang Dian’s flash fiction collection and A History of Chinese Philosophical Thought by Zhang Xianghao.
